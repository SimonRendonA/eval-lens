# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback gracefully and give it kindly
- Focus on what's best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **conduct@rendonarango.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
